Vision & Mission

Our Vision

QHedge aims to uphold our position as Qatar's top Islamic hedge fund, and the world’s next leading Shariah Compliant trust with unmatched investing solutions for our customers.

Our Mission

  • Expand Across the Gulf: Extend our operations and influence the asset management industry strategically across the Gulf region.
  • Customer’s satisfaction: Cultivate a client-centric culture that places the needs and satisfaction of our esteemed clientele at the forefront of our operations.
  • Employee Dedication: Attract, develop, and retain qualified top-tier industry professionals who are dedicated to delivering excellent client service and innovative investment strategies.
  • Innovative Sharia-Compliant Strategies: Continue to invest in and leverage state-of-the-art technology solutions, such as Aladdin®, to set industry standards.
  • Ethical and Transparent Practices: Uphold the highest ethical standards of Islamic finance, avoiding speculative practices and excessive uncertainty.
  • Commitment to Charitable Causes: Dedicate a portion of profits to charitable endeavors and community welfare in line with Islamic finance's principles.
  • Adaptability and Growth: Navigate market dynamics and economic uncertainties while seeking opportunities for growth and excellence.
  • Global Macro Strategy: Build investments around a global macro strategy, considering economic, social, and technological trends.
  • Focus on Fundamental Value: Concentrate on fundamental value investments over momentum, always monitor and update investment strategies but adhering to a strict investment discipline.

Values

  • Trust: We prioritize trust earned from all stakeholders.
  • Ethical Leadership: We adhere to the highest ethical standards, prioritize client interests, and promote ethical leadership for our fund's success while ensuring Shariah compliance.
  • Diligence: We execute tasks with undivided attention to detail and precision.
  • Risk Management: We rigorously manage risks to safeguard clients' capital and investments, all within the bounds of Shariah compliance.
  • Client-Centric Sustainability: Our focus is on tailoring services to our clients' financial goals, aligning prosperity with Shariah principles, and contributing to Qatar's long-term economic development and stability.
